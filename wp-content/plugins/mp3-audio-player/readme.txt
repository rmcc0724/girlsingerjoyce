== Description ==

= Plugin's demo. =

Do not waste time, check if this [plugin's demo](http://pluginlyspeaking.com/demo-mp3-audio-player/) meets your needs.

= Broadcast your music. =

This plugin gives you the possibility to broadcast some music thanks to an Audio Player or Directly from the Background.

You’ll be able to easily choose your music, your audio player style, your settings and just add a shortcode.

The goal of the plugin is to let you choose between two features : Audio Player or Background Music, and then broadcast your audio files.

= How to use our plugin ? =

* You just have to download your plugin and upload it to your "wp-content/plugins" folder.
* Activate your plugin on your Dashboard and you will see an Audio Player tabs.
* At this point, you just have to create a new Audio Player by completing the fields.
* Once created, go back to the list of your Audio Player and you will see a Shortcode.
* All you have to do, is put the Shortcode wherever you want to get your Audio Player / Background Music.
